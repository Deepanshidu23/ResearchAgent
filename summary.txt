The current roadmap of Ai Engineering includes the development of more advanced machine learning algorithms, increased use of deep learning, and the integration of Ai with other technologies such as IoT and blockchain. Additionally, there is a growing focus on the ethics and transparency of Ai systems, as well as the need for more diverse and representative training data. The roadmap also includes the development of more autonomous systems, such as self-driving cars and drones, and the use of Ai in areas such as healthcare and education.
